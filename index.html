Non-QM Loans

Self-Employed · ITIN · Investors · 1099

Don't fit a traditional box? Non-QM is built for you. Qualify using bank statements, rental income, assets, or ITIN — no W-2 required.

  • Bank Statement Loans (12 or 24 months)
  • DSCR — qualify on rental income only
  • ITIN Loans — no SSN required
  • Foreign National programs
  • Asset Depletion / 1099 income
  • No tax returns needed
